# Design System

## Overview

Ace Magnates uses a dark premium finance aesthetic built on deep black surfaces, burgundy-red glow, and restrained gold highlights. The homepage is organized around a tall hero statement, a floating portfolio card, six feature cards, a compact stats grid, testimonial cards, and a final CTA box. Typography mixes a dramatic serif headline voice with a clean sans-serif body, giving the brand both luxury and clarity. Overall the site feels like a high-end community pitch: confident, exclusive, and slightly cinematic.

## Colors

- **Primary Surface**: `#0a0a0a` - near-black page background.
- **Secondary Surface**: `#111111` - card fill and overlays.
- **Surface Lift**: `#1a1a1a` - hover state and deeper card tone.
- **Primary Red**: `#c41e2e` - main brand accent, CTA, active states.
- **Primary Red Light**: `#e0333f` - brighter red highlight and glow edge.
- **Primary Red Dark**: `#9a1520` - shadowed crimson depth.
- **Accent Gold**: `#d4a853` - premium highlight and luxury accents.
- **Accent Silver**: `#c0c0c0` - muted stat and border support.
- **Text Primary**: `#ffffff` - major headlines and key numbers.
- **Text Secondary**: `#b0b0b0` - supporting copy and labels.

## Typography

- **Display Serif**: `Playfair Display` - hero lines, brand mark, luxury emphasis.
- **Sans Serif**: `Inter` - body copy, labels, stats, and UI text.
- **Hierarchy**: hero headlines are oversized and high-contrast; stats are bold and tabular; body text stays readable and airy.

## Elevation

The site uses glassmorphism on top of dark gradients: blurred cards, thin red-tinted borders, soft inner highlights, and subtle glow shadows. Depth comes from layered blobs and background wash rather than hard drop shadows. The card language is rounded, polished, and softly reflective.

## Components

- **Luxury Navbar**: compact logo lockup, thin active underline, and a red CTA pill.
- **Hero Statement**: oversized serif headline with red gradient brand name.
- **Portfolio Card**: dark glass panel with growth number, percent change, and tiny chart bars.
- **Feature Cards**: six rounded cards with icon, title, and short explanation.
- **Stats Grid**: bold number blocks with gradient emphasis.
- **Testimonial Cards**: dark proof cards with star row and author lockup.
- **CTA Box**: centered closing panel with strong button hierarchy.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's

- Use near-black backgrounds with red and gold accents.
- Keep typography large, elegant, and readable.
- Favor glass panels, soft borders, and gentle glow.
- Let numbers feel important and premium.

### Don'ts

- Do not use bright white canvases.
- Do not use blue or purple as the lead accent.
- Do not make the layout feel like a standard dashboard.
- Do not reduce the serif headline to a generic sans.
